Overview

The Analog Data Logger is a 4-channel wireless device designed for unattended, long-term monitoring. It converts analog sensor signals into digital data with high accuracy and transmits them via secure LoRa networks. Configurable through mobile or cloud applications, it simplifies deployment, reduces maintenance, and ensures reliable data collection for bridges, tunnels, buildings, and industrial assets.

What's Included

Robust hardware and intelligent services designed to deliver precise analog measurements and seamless data collection from the very first deployment.

  • 4-channel analog input with multiple input types
  • Sensor power outputs: 5 V, 12 V, 24 V (up to 60 mA per channel)
  • Weatherproof design (IP68)
  • Mobile app for field setup, voltage thresholds, and wiring assistance
  • Long battery life of up to 10 years depending on configuration
  • Configurable reporting periods and automated data transmission
  • Monthly Performance Reports
