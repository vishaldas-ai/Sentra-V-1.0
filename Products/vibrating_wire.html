Overview

Sentra’s Vibrating Wire Data Loggers bring automation and reliability to structural and geotechnical monitoring. By wirelessly integrating piezometers, strain gauges, pressure cells, and load cells, they eliminate manual data handling and deliver accurate, continuous measurements to your central system.
