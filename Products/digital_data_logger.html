Overview

The Digital Data Logger is a robust, low-power device designed for long-term, continuous monitoring of geotechnical, structural, and environmental sensors. It leverages intelligent edge processing to automatically collect, process, and transmit sensor data wirelessly, supporting threshold alerts and seamless integration via ModBus RTU and other protocols — eliminating the need for manual data retrieval, even in remote or isolated locations.
