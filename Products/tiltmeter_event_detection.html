Overview

The Sentra Tiltmeter — Event Detection edition is a compact, 3-axis inclinometer engineered to detect and report ground movements with low noise and fast latency. It runs continuous sampling in normal operation and switches to high-frequency capture when preset thresholds are exceeded, streaming event data to gateways and cloud platforms for rapid assessment and response. Configuration may be done locally via mobile tools or remotely through Sentra’s edge/cloud management layers.
