Overview

The Sentra K20 Edge Repeater strengthens wireless sensor networks by boosting signal reach and maintaining continuous communication between sensors and gateways. Ideal for challenging terrains, tunnels, or extended infrastructure projects, it ensures that critical tilt, vibration, and GNSS data is transmitted reliably. With built-in edge processing, the repeater can manage event-based triggers and locally buffer data when network connectivity is temporarily unavailable.
