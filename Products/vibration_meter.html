Overview

The Vibration Meter is a wireless, tri-axial sensor built for long-term, continuous vibration monitoring of bridges, tunnels, buildings, and industrial assets. It leverages an exception-based edge algorithm that automatically detects and reports threshold breaches based on Law/PPV and frequency parameters, eliminating the need for manual data retrieval.
