Overview

The Thread X3 is a high-performance broadband node built for demanding monitoring environments. It facilitates secure, high-speed data transmission from multiple sensors, including cameras, total stations, and multiparameter instruments. Advanced relay capabilities and local processing ensure critical alerts are maintained even in temporary network outages.
