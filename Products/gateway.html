Overview

The Sentra 4G Rugged Gateway collects and forwards data from remote sensors, including tiltmeters, GNSS meters, and vibration meters, to cloud platforms or local servers. Designed for harsh environments, it ensures reliable communication even in areas without stable wired networks. Built-in edge intelligence processes events and manages data locally when connectivity is limited, guaranteeing seamless monitoring of infrastructure, tunnels, slopes, and industrial assets.
