As infrastructure owners push to cut risk and extend asset life, digital twins paired with edge AI are emerging as the fastest way to get real-time visibility and predictive insight into structures. Digital twins can improve capital and operational efficiency for public infrastructure by roughly 20–30%.

Shifting AI inference to the edge enables instant anomaly detection from distributed sensors (GNSS, tiltmeters, vibration nodes), producing low-latency alerts so teams can act before small issues escalate.At the same time, private 5G / non-public networks and growing predictive-maintenance platforms are providing the reliable, low-latency connectivity and analytics ecosystems needed for live digital twins and large sensor fleets — accelerating on-site deployments worldwide.

After deploying Sentra’s GNSS meters and edge gateways, our team gained continuous, high-fidelity visibility into structural behaviour. Their digital-twin dashboards and predictive alerts reduced unplanned maintenance, improved planning accuracy, and materially increased asset reliability — a true step-change for our operations.

Sidharth Kamasani

CEO of SENTRA

“Sentra’s edge-powered monitoring and clear dashboards transformed our inspection program. We get precise alerts, meaningful trends, and actionable reports that help us prevent issues before they escalate.”
