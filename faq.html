Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

We specialize in structural health monitoring and IoT-based solutions, including GNSS monitoring, tiltmeters, vibration sensors, gateways, data loggers, and real-time analytics platforms. Our end-to-end services help organizations ensure infrastructure safety, optimize maintenance, and make smarter, data-driven decisions.

Some monitoring benefits, like real-time alerts from sensors, are immediate once the system is deployed. However, deeper insights—such as trend analysis, predictive maintenance, and structural health assessments—typically become clearer within 3–6 months of continuous data collection.

Our technology is designed for infrastructure, construction, railways, mining, energy, and smart cities. Any industry that relies on critical structures or assets can benefit from Sentra’s real-time monitoring and predictive insights.

All monitoring systems use encrypted communication protocols, secure gateways, and cloud-based data protection. We prioritize data integrity and confidentiality, ensuring compliance with international standards.

Our team works closely with you to assess your infrastructure, risk factors, and monitoring goals. Whether you need real-time alerts, long-term structural health monitoring, or large-scale IoT deployment, we recommend the right combination of sensors, gateways, and analytics tailored to your project. Every solution is customized to fit your asset type, scale, and budget.
